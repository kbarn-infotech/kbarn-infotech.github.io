Our Favorite Brands

At Mindful Mixer, we take pride in partnering with brands that share our passion for quality, innovation, and mindful drinking.

Here are some of our favorite brands that bring exceptional value to our nonalcoholic cocktail creations:

  1. Seedlip: Pioneers in Nonalcoholic Spirits

    Seedlip is the world’s first distilled nonalcoholic spirit, designed to offer sophisticated and complex flavors without the alcohol. With their beautifully crafted blends inspired by nature, Seedlip's spirits elevate any drink to a new level of elegance and taste.

  2. Lyre's: Impeccable Nonalcoholic Spirits

    Lyre's range of nonalcoholic spirits is crafted to taste just like the classics we all know and love. From their Dry London Spirit to their American Malt, Lyre's delivers premium flavors, allowing you to enjoy your favorite cocktails without the alcohol.

  3. Fever-Tree: Premium Mixers for Every Occasion

    Fever-Tree's premium mixers are the perfect companion to any nonalcoholic spirit. Made with the finest natural ingredients, Fever-Tree tonics, sodas, and ginger ales enhance the flavor profiles of our cocktails, making every sip a delightful experience.

  4. Ritual Zero Proof: Redefining Nonalcoholic Drinks

    Ritual Zero Proof creates nonalcoholic alternatives that are bold, flavorful, and perfect for any occasion. Their meticulously crafted spirits, including Whiskey Alternative and Gin Alternative, provide all the complexity and warmth of traditional spirits, minus the alcohol.

These brands are more than just names to us—they represent a commitment to excellence, innovation, and mindful enjoyment. We’re proud to feature them in our creations and share their extraordinary products with you. Cheers to discovering new favorites!
