"Gin" & Tonic

Ingredients
1 cup water 1 cup sugar 1 small cucumber (sliced)
1 tsp dried lavender 1 tbsp juniper berries (crushed) 1 tsp coriander seeds
1 tsp black peppercorns 1 tsp cardamom pods 1 tsp fennel seeds
1/2 lemon (sliced) A few sprigs of fresh rosemary Tonic water
Instructions:
  1. Make the Juniper Syrup:
    1. In a saucepan, combine the water, sugar, juniper berries, coriander seeds, cardamom pods, fennel seeds, and black peppercorns
    2. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.
    3. Remove from heat and let it cool. Strain the mixture to remove solids, keeping the infused syrup.
  2. Infuse the Syrup:
    1. In a large jar, combine the cooled juniper syrup with the cucumber slices, lemon slices, dried lavender, and fresh rosemary.
    2. Seal the jar and let it infuse in the fridge for at least 24 hours

  3. Serve:

    To serve, fill a glass with ice, pour in the juniper syrup mixture to taste (start with a couple of tablespoons), and top off with tonic water. Stir gently and garnish with a slice of cucumber, lemon, and a sprig of rosemary.
Enjoy your Gin-free G&T!
